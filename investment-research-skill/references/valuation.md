# Valuation

## Before Valuation

Do not value a company until business quality, balance sheet safety, and earnings quality are understood.

If cash flow is unpredictable or accounting is unreliable, state `无法可靠估值`.

## Methods

Use the method that fits the business:

- Free cash flow discounting: stable cash-generative companies.
- Owner earnings: high-quality long-term compounders.
- Relative valuation: comparable companies with similar accounting and cycle position.
- Asset value: financials, real estate, resources, or liquidation-like cases.

## Scenario Requirements

Always include:

- Bear case.
- Base case.
- Bull case.

For each case, specify:

- Revenue growth.
- Margin or ROIC assumption.
- Reinvestment or capex needs.
- Terminal assumption or exit multiple.
- Resulting value range.

## Margin of Safety

Required margin depends on certainty:

- Very high certainty: 20-30%.
- Good but not exceptional: 30-40%.
- Material uncertainty: 40-50%.
- Cannot estimate: do not buy.

## Output

Valuation output must include:

- Current market cap and price if available.
- Key assumptions.
- Intrinsic value range.
- Acceptable buy price.
- Current margin of safety.
- Main reason the valuation may be wrong.
